Explainer

OpenAI, as a private company, has a growing number of strategic partnerships and collaborations with publicly traded companies across various sectors, particularly for its AI infrastructure, training data, and product integration. Based on recent announcements, some of the publicly traded companies with direct and significant cooperation with OpenAI include: Microsoft (MSFT): Microsoft is a primary strategic partner and investor in OpenAI, with a multibillion-dollar investment. Their collaboration involves using OpenAI's models in Microsoft's Azure cloud service and integrating AI features like ChatGPT into products such as Bing. Advanced Micro Devices (AMD): AMD has announced a strategic partnership and a significant agreement to supply its Instinct GPUs to power OpenAI's next-generation AI infrastructure. NVIDIA (NVDA): NVIDIA has a longstanding and deep collaboration with OpenAI, providing GPUs that are fundamental to AI training. They have also entered into a strategic partnership for deploying a massive amount of NVIDIA's AI systems. Oracle (ORCL): OpenAI has engaged in strategic partnerships, reportedly valued at billions, with Oracle, likely related to cloud computing and AI datacenter infrastructure. Apple (AAPL): OpenAI and Apple announced a partnership to integrate ChatGPT across Apple's operating systems (iOS, iPadOS, macOS) and tools like Siri as part of the Apple Intelligence initiative. SoftBank: SoftBank has been named in strategic partnerships with OpenAI, particularly concerning the expansion of AI infrastructure and the "Stargate" initiative. OpenAI also has other significant collaborations that involve product integration or data licensing, which include: Shutterstock (SSTK): Collaborated with OpenAI, allowing OpenAI to use its image library for training models like DALL-E. Salesforce (CRM): Has integrated OpenAI technology, such as with its Slack platform and EinsteinGPT. Expedia (EXPE): Named as a launch partner for OpenAI's "App SDK," allowing users to access their apps through ChatGPT. Uber (UBER): Named as an upcoming partner for the App SDK integration. DoorDash (DASH): Named as an upcoming partner for the App SDK integration. Etsy (ETSY) and Shopify (SHOP): Announced as having an "Instant Checkout" feature in ChatGPT. Spotify (SPOT): Has a partnership, though it emphasizes not sharing user data with OpenAI to train models. Zillow (Z): Announced as a collaboration partner. News Corp (NWS) and Reddit (RDDT): Have content licensing and partnership deals with OpenAI to enhance ChatGPT with their content and explore AI-powered features. Coursera (COUR): Closed higher after a mention from OpenAI's DevDay.
